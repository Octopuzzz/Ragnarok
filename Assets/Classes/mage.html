MAGE


Mages rely on their skills as their main source of damage, and such attacks are magically based and only take into account the target's Magical Defense, and not their Physical Defense.

Furthermore, all of a Mage's spells are elemental, thus meaning the accurate use of the correct spell against the correct monsters is highly important.


Mage Skills

Skill Description
Seal Explosion Seal Explosion detonates the currently applied seal effect to obtain a new effect.
Ice Wall Ice Wall forms a wall of ice that blocks all enemy attacks for 10 seconds. Cannot move or use other skills while behind the Ice Wall.
Inferno Inferno creates a blazing inferno to inflict Magic damage to a max of 10 enemies within a 7m radius, setting the area on fire with the Firestarter effect. Damage is split amongst all enemies affected.
Flame Explosion 100 Pyromaniac required. Leap into the air and shoot fireballs at the enemy to inflict Fire damage and Stun for 3 seconds. Seal Explosion during effect duration, this skill will become a critical hit.
Meteor Storm Meteor Storm summons a meteor from the sky to inflict damage to a max of 10 enemies within the targeted area. This skill is most effective when used on more than 3 enemies, however, the damage is reduced when used on more than 6 enemies.
